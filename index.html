About Us

The American Companies has provided a high quality, cost-effective level of service to the transportation community for over 100 years - our flagship business, American Shipping Company, was originally founded in 1899. As a Nationally Permitted U.S. Customs Broker & Export Foreign Freight Forwarder, we participate in all phases of the Automated Broker Interface (ACE, RLF, ACH, AES, AMS, CSI, ISF, reconciliation and all other programs), enabling us to custom clear merchandise or export your cargo as quickly and efficiently as possible.

News Feeds Heading

Although established as a freight forwarder, handling internationally bound cargo in the New York Hudson River area, our focus on providing seamless product and information flow has helped us to grow to the point where we now offer an entire suite of complementary services - Customs Broker, Freight Forwarding, Logistics operations, Trucking and Warehousing.

With our strong emphasis on compliance, we have been recognized as a leader in such programs as the Importer Security Filing initiative. All of our locations are staffed with licensed Customs Brokers that offer years of expertise that professionally represent our customers and meet all regulatory requirements.

We Import Superior quality of KD Lumber & logs from Europe, USA, Canada, Africa and Myanmar. Logs: American Walnut, Cherry, White Oak, Red Oak, White Ash. African Logs & Lumber: Wenge, Mahogany, Iroko, Perpal Heart, Bubinga, Hickory. Indian Species: Silver Oak, Rose Wood, Mahogany, White Cider, Rubber Wood. For Exterior Cladding: Thermo Treated Beech, Ash & IPE.
